On the walls, beneath the streets

A platform for independent publishing, printing, and creative support—for passion, not profit.

Brought to life by two explorers based in England, Subliminal Press exists to help bring your work into the physical world. In a time when so much art, writing, and photography lives only on screens, we want to ensure it can be held, seen, and appreciated in real life. We don’t sell work, and we don’t take a cut—we’re just here to help make it happen.

Publications & Printed Matter

We assist in the creation of independent publications—books, zines, and other printed works. From design and layout to coordinating printing with trusted local presses in England, we’re here to help. Financial support is available to ensure that cost isn’t a barrier to putting your work into print and into the hands of those who will love it.

Photographic Printing & Framing

For photographers, we facilitate high-quality printing and framing in England. Whether you’re producing a limited edition series or a single framed piece, we can help coordinate the details so your work is presented exactly as it should be—on the walls of those who value it.

Websites & Digital Presence

We help advise and build simple, effective websites to showcase your projects, making them accessible to the right audience.
