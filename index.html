On the walls, beneath the streets

A platform for independent publishing, printing, and creative support - for passion, not profit.

Subpress is an initiative started by two friends and explorers, dedicated to helping artists bring their work into the real world. In a time when so much art, writing, and photography exists only on screens, we want to ensure it’s seen, held, and appreciated. We’re not in this for profit - we just want to make cool shit happen. If we charge, it’s only to cover costs.

Printed Matter

We assist in the creation of independent publications like books, zines, and whatever vision you have. From design and layout to coordinating local printers, we want to make it happen. Financial support is available to ensure that cost isn’t a barrier to putting your work into print.

Display

We can facilitate high-quality printing and framing in England. Whether it’s a limited edition series or a one-off for a mate, we’ll make sure it’s presented exactly as it should be - on the walls of those who value it.

Digital

We help create simple, effective websites to showcase your work and connect it with the right people.
