What is the yearbook?

A yearly collection of pictures and stories that showcase the global exploring community's creativity and insanity. It captures who was around and what we did - the parties, the daring infiltrations, the cruisy explores, and everything inbetween - rather than amazing pics of people standing in tunnels (though a few of those do get in).

Please note that as much as I try to include everyone, due to submission volume and page-count I simply can't. To maximise your chances of being included, read these guidelines and ask questions if need be. Going through past Yearbooks will also give you an idea of what I like.

Photo requirements

* If the image accompanies a story it can be included without these.

- Taken between 1st of Jan 2023 and 31st Dec 2023;

- Up to 100 images;

- Visibly exploring related*;

- People in frame*;

- Photos you have personally taken;

- Phone / DSLR / Film photos are all fine;

But please, 1000px+ on one side absolute minimum.

- Faces uncensored;

Faces are blurred on request, but I like to make them all the same style.

Story requirements

- As a file, eg. google doc / word / notepad;

- Max 3,000 words;

- No names or location details if it's spicy;

- Up to 2 stories per contributor;

- At least one image per story.

If you've got a story to tell but aren't a confident writer, get in touch and we can workshop something.

Ordering

Price: $60 AUD + shipping

Softcover, 280-300 pages, limited edition of under 200 copies.

All contributors have a book put aside by default, but if you need to order multiple copies or aren't submitting, send an email to lodge a pre-order.
