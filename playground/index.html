About — LDN

You were somewhere
last night. We want
to put it in print.

Subpress publishes work from the underground — graff, urbex, subculture, the places and people mainstream publishing won't touch. We handle everything: design, layout, print. We don't need to know the details. We just need to see the work.

Cost is never the reason it doesn't happen.
